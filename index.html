About the Station

Bluegrass Radio from a Real Bluegrass Farm

Built by a working farm family, this station keeps the spirit of front-porch picking, church-house singing, and country storytelling alive on the air.

Adam Rideout’s Blue Grass Farm Radio is an online station dedicated to traditional and modern bluegrass, classic country, and a touch of gospel and Americana. Broadcasted live through Live365 / Live 360, you can tune in from anywhere with a phone, tablet, or computer.

From old-time fiddle tunes to new-grass arrangements, the goal is simple: play music that feels like sitting on the porch after a long day’s work, with good friends and a cool breeze through the trees.

  • 24/7 stream hosted on Live365 (Live 360 platform).
  • Family-friendly programming and lyrics.
  • Mix of classic legends and independent artists.
  • Listener requests and shout-outs welcome.
